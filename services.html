If you are confident about your exact time of birth, I will create your natal chart to provide detailed insights tailored to your situation. However, if you are unsure of your birth time — which is quite common, even among those who believe they know it — there is no disadvantage. In fact, this can be an advantage. In such cases, I use a specialized method called a Horary Chart. Horary Charts are highly reliable and precise, offering accurate answers without the need for birth time details.

Questions generally fall under categories such as career, relationships, childbirth, investments, education, and health. Please click on the buttons provided on this page to see example questions under these categories, which can help you frame your own query. If your question doesn’t fall under these categories, feel free to frame it in a way that clearly communicates your concerns.

Once your question is ready and you have made the payment, kindly share the proof of payment on WhatsApp. We will coordinate a suitable time for a brief discussion to fully understand your query and current situation. After this initial discussion, I will construct the applicable chart and provide the answers within 1–2 business days or sooner, depending on my schedule.
